 Peer-to-Peer Marketplace App 
 At  TradeHub  Platforms,  individuals  struggle  to  buy  and  sell  second-hand  goods  efficiently  due  to  lack  of  trust,  poor  listing  systems,  and  limited  communication  channels.  To  enable  seamless transactions, we require a Flutter-based Peer-to-Peer Marketplace App. 
 The  app  should  allow  users  to  list  products  with  images,  descriptions,  and  pricing.  Buyers  can  search,  filter,  and  contact  sellers  directly  via  in-app  chat.  The  system  should  include  ratings and reviews to build trust among users. 
 Additionally,  secure  payment  options  and  order  tracking  should  be  provided  for  a  complete  marketplace  experience.  The  app  must  support  offline  browsing  of  cached  listings  and  sync  updates when online. 
 Key Pain Points to Solve: 
●	Lack of trust in peer transactions. 
●	Poor product discovery mechanisms. 
●	Inefficient communication between buyers and sellers. 
●	Limited access to secure payment systems.
